We are a dedicated team of dermatology professionals committed to providing the highest standard of care for your skin, hair, and nails. Our philosophy blends compassionate healing with innovative medical solutions to ensure every patient receives personalized and effective treatment.

Dermatology is the branch of medicine dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of conditions related to the skin, hair, and nails. It addresses a wide range of issues, from common conditions like acne and eczema to complex diseases like skin cancer, providing both medical and surgical solutions to maintain the health and appearance of the body's largest organ.

DermoSys Guidelines

Patient Registration

Create comprehensive patient profiles by capturing demographics, vitals, initial complaints, and medical history. The system automatically calculates BMI and BSA, assigning a unique patient code for easy tracking.

Follow-up Management

For returning patients, record new observations and track progress chronologically. Update treatment plans and examination findings through the "Patient Visit" feature to maintain a continuous care record.

Digital Prescriptions

Generate and manage digital prescriptions with ease. Select from a list of medications, specify dosage and frequency, and set a "Next Follow-up Date" to automate patient adherence tracking.

Dashboard Analytics

Utilize the main dashboard for a real-time overview of key clinical statistics. Monitor patient trends with interactive charts, track missed follow-ups, and review detailed staff activity via the user audit trail.

Lab & Image Management

Centralize all diagnostic materials. Upload clinical photos directly to patient profiles, request external radiology scans, and manage lab reports to create a complete visual and diagnostic history.

In-Patient Bed Allocation

Efficiently manage admitted patients by assigning them to available beds. Record daily progress notes and process discharge summaries to ensure a seamless and well-documented in-patient workflow.
